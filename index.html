Our Story

More than coffee.
It's a ritual.

Brewed & Co. started as a simple dream: a neighborhood café where people could slow down. We opened our doors in 2018 with a small menu, a used espresso machine, and a deep love for good coffee.

Today we source directly from farmers in the Cordillera highlands, roast in small batches twice a week, and obsess over water temperature and grind size so you don't have to. You just enjoy the cup.
